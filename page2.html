Brazilian Manners

Brazilian Manners is an instructive code to simplify and encourage a greeting that creates a fundamental base to the Latin culture.

  1. For this program to run it requires two people.
  2. To begin stand up in an upright form and face your partner at a distance of 5 meter.
  3. Once facing each other, partner A, you will approach partner B in a steady “walking pace”.
  4. At 1 meter, partner A will extend their right arm while continuing to approach partner B. Aiming for just above partner b’s shoulder and with its head leaning towards the left.
  5. Once a foot is between partners chests, partner A will tilt forearm to a slow rest in the middle of partner b’s back.
  6. Partner A must then lean head in aiming over Partner B’s left cheek. Partner B must follow along with a 30 degree tilt of head.
  7. Partner A then presses his right cheek on partner B’s left cheek. While both imitate the act of a kiss.
